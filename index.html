RABBIT REPUBLIC
A GAME OF SELECTIVE PRESSURE & SURVIVAL
AUSTRALIA, 1859.

Twenty-four wild rabbits have been released onto the estate of Thomas Austin in Victoria. He wanted something to hunt.

What follows will become one of the greatest ecological disasters in human history. The continent is vast. The grasslands are empty. There are no wolves, no foxes, no diseases that know your name — yet.

Your goal: spread across every habitable zone in Australia. 30 seasons. Every choice changes your population's genetics forever. Tasmania, Lord Howe Island, and Norfolk Island await the boldest.
